### R-Column

ラ (ra) : **Ro**cking Chair

リ (ri) : Same as Hiragana **り** (River)

ル (ru) : **Roo**ts of a Tree

レ (re) : **Ra**zor Blade

ロ (ro) : **Ro**bot's Mouth
