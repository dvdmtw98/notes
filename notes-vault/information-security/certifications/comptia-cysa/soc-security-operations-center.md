A location from were security professionals monitor and protect critical information assets in an organization.  
SOC looks for IOC (Indicators of Compromise) in logs.  
Smaller organizations outsource SOC to MSSP.  

Essential for Efficient Functioning:
- Have the authority to operate. Power provided by organizational policies.  
- Have motivated and skilled professionals.  
- Incorporate processes into a single center.  
- Equipped to perform incident response.  
- Protect itself and the organization at large.  
- Can separate the signal from the noise.  
- Collaborate with other SOCs for data sharing.  

SOC should be the single point of contact for security, monitoring and incident response.
