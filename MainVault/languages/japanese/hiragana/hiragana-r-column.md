### R-Column

ら (ra) : **Ra**bbit

り (ri) : **Ri**ver

る (ru) : Hand holding a **Ru**by

れ (re) : **Re**indeer looking up

ろ (ro) : **Ro**bbed (of Ruby)

---

### Example Words

* さる
	* SaRu (Monkey)
* はれ
	* HaRe (Sunny/ Clear Weather)
* らく
	* RaKu (Convenient/ Conformable)
* おふろ
	* OFuRo (Bath)
* くすり
	* KuSuRi (Medicine)
* おにぎり
	* ONiGiRi (Onigiri - Japanese Disk)
* ぬいぐるみ
	* NuIGuRuMi (Stuffed Toy)
