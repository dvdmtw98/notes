### K-Column

カ (ka) : Angular Hiragana **か** without the floating stroke (Blade cutting Stick)  
ガ (ga)

キ (ki) : Similar to Hiragana **き** without the bottom part (Key)  
ギ (gi)

ク (ku) : **Cu**ckoo's Tail  
グ (gu)

ケ (ke) : Sideways **K**  
ゲ (ge)

コ (ko) : Road with 2 **Co**rners  
ゴ (go)
